Pancakes

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 2
Baking Powder 2 tsp

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add milk and eggs, whisking until smooth.
  3. Heat a frying pan and add a little butter.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and cook until golden brown on both sides.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Spaghetti 200g
Ground Beef 300g
Tomato Sauce 1 cup
Garlic 2 cloves

Instructions

  • Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  • In a pan, brown the ground beef and garlic.
  • Add tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Serve sauce over spaghetti and enjoy!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Chocolate Chips 1 cup
Butter 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat on oven.
  2. Mix butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add flour and chocolate chips, stirring to combine.
  4. Place spoonfuls of dough on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
